## Level 0
![[Level 0.png]]
A wireless local area network. The PC below sends an image to switch who then turns the image into pieces and the router transmits the image to router (above) back to switch for rebuilding of an image.

![[Level 0 Lan.png]]

An Local Area Network, thanks to the switch in the middle four computers can talk to each other and each 'host' (computers) have a hostname and IP address that uniquely identifies each other so the switch knows who is the sender and the receiver.
